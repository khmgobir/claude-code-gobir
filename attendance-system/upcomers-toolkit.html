Rules reference — verified from Upcomers' help center

The mechanics that actually decide payouts and breaches. Read these before trading a funded account.

Dynamic Risk Shield™ (trailing drawdown)

A drawdown that follows your highest equity (high-water mark) in real time, sitting the plan's % below it (Vanguard 7%, Oracle 5%, Ember 4%, Nova sprints 3%). Calculated on equity, not balance — open trades count.

It locks at your initial balance once your profit reaches the shield %, then becomes static and stops trailing — you can run profits freely after that.

⚠ Withdrawal trap: taking out profits does NOT reset the shield. After it locks, if you withdraw down to your shield floor, balance = drawdown limit and the account can breach instantly. Leave a buffer above the floor.

Best Day Rule / consistency (soft)

Best Day % = highest single-day profit ÷ total profit × 100. Cap is 20% (some accounts 15%). Tracked live in your dashboard — and in this tool's Consistency meters.

This is a soft rule: exceeding it delays your payout until profit is spread more evenly across days. It does not breach the account.

Sprints use a Best Trade Rule instead (Supernova/Hypernova 15%, Ultranova 25% consistency) on a single trade rather than a day.

Hard breaches (these end the account)

Single-trade-loss over the cap (Vanguard/Oracle 2%, Ember 1.5%) is a hard breach. So is hitting the Risk Shield floor or a daily-loss limit (4% on Vanguard/Oracle/Supernova/Hypernova; none on Ember/Ultranova).

The daily-march rule (each valid day must close ≥ +0.5% realized) is a counting rule, not a breach — a flat/negative day just doesn't count toward your valid-day total.

Payouts & cap progression

99% profit split (accounts after 13 Dec 2025), paid from the first payout. Request anytime; processing ~12h, often under an hour.

Every payout needs ≥ 1% profit on the funded balance first ($1,000 on a $100k account), and it resets each time.

Payout cap rises step-by-step: a fully approved withdrawal bumps the cap up a level; partial/declined leaves it unchanged. After the 8th payout, unlimited cap by individual agreement. The Nova sprints are single-session — one payout on the final result, no tiered cap.
